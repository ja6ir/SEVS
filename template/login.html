About

SEVS for JB is a secure electronic voting platform designed for JaiBharath College elections. We ensure privacy, accuracy, and transparency through cutting-edge encryption, voter authentication, and end-to-end verification, all while adhering to the university's election protocols. Our system guarantees secure, auditable, and trustworthy elections for the student body.
