SEVS for JB: A Secure E-Voting System for JaiBharath College

A secure and transparent electronic voting system designed exclusively for JaiBharath College to facilitate a smooth, confidential, and accurate election process that adheres to our university’s protocols.
Our platform leverages state-of-the-art cryptographic solutions, such as homomorphic encryption and zero-knowledge proofs, ensuring privacy-preserving vote tallying and verifiable, tamper-resistant voting. With a focus on end-to-end verification, secure voter authentication, and an auditable paper trail, SEVS for JB guarantees that your vote is both secure and accountable, while following the strict guidelines set by JaiBharath College's electoral process.



What We Do

Empowering Secure Elections with Advanced Encryption and Trust.

Secure Voting Process

We use cutting-edge cryptographic methods like homomorphic encryption to protect vote privacy and ensure that each vote remains secure and untampered.

End-to-End Verification

Our system enables voters to verify their votes, ensuring that every vote is correctly recorded, counted, and traceable without compromising privacy.

Audit and Compliance

We generate an auditable paper trail for each vote and follow JaiBharath College's election protocols to ensure integrity, compliance, and accountability in every election.
